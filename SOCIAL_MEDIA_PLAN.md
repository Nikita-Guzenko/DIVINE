# Divine Enterprises Social Media Plan

## LinkedIn

**Goal:** Attract experienced CDL-A drivers + brand awareness

### Content Mix (3-4 posts/week):

**For Drivers (60%):**
- Day in the life of a team driver at Divine
- Earnings: realistic team driver income
- Team vs solo advantages (less downtime, more miles)
- "We train" — for those who just got their CDL
- Driver stories (photos with trucks)

**Brand Awareness / Clients (25%):**
- Routes we cover
- Delivery reliability
- Fleet and equipment

**Engagement (15%):**
- Polls: "Solo or Team — what do you prefer?"
- Questions: "What's your longest haul?"
- Industry news + opinion

### Outreach Strategy:
- Search for people with "CDL-A", "Truck Driver", "OTR Driver" in profile
- Connect + personalized message
- Comment on posts in trucking groups

---

## Twitter/X

**Goal:** Active presence in trucking community

### Content (5-7 posts/week):
- Short tips for drivers
- Job openings (short and clear)
- Retweets of industry content + comments
- **Memes/humor about trucking life** — *OPTIONAL IF COMPANY APPROVES (potential virality)*

### Active Outreach:

**Groups/accounts to monitor:**
- @TruckersNews
- @CDaborhah (trucking influencers)
- #TruckDriver #CDL #TruckerLife hashtags

**Tactics:**
- Comment on posts where drivers complain about employers
- Reply to "looking for work" posts
- DM people who post about looking for team partner

---

## Key Metrics to Track:
- Follower growth
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- DM response rate
- Candidates sourced from social media
