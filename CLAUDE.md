# RECRUITING SYSTEM

## CRITICAL RULE

**TEAM DRIVERS PREFERRED, SOLO DRIVERS ACCEPTED!**
Divine operates with teams (2 drivers per truck). Solo drivers who are willing to work in a team can apply — we provide partner matching.

## Business Context

- **Client:** Divine Enterprises
- **Task:** Recruit CDL Team Drivers
- **Contract:** $1,200/month base + $760 per hired team
- **Goal:** 5 teams/month = $5,000

## Architecture

```
Gmail (CareerPlug notifications)
    |
    v
Playwright (browser scraping CareerPlug)
    |
    v
SQLite Database (data/candidates.db) <-- source of truth
    |
    v
Pre-screening Email --> Team OK / Solo Only
    |
    v
Call only "Team OK" candidates
    |
    v
Google Sheet (sync for work tracking)
```

## Workflow

| Step | Command | Result |
|------|---------|--------|
| 1 | `python run.py process` | Import candidates from Gmail/CareerPlug |
| 2 | `python run.py screen` | Send pre-screening email |
| 3 | `python run.py check-replies` | Parse responses, update statuses |
| 4 | `python run.py list --status "Team OK"` | List candidates ready for call |
| 5 | Call candidates | Manual step |
| 6 | `python run.py sync` | Sync to Google Sheet |

## Candidate Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `New` | Not processed yet |
| `Screening` | Pre-screening email sent, awaiting response |
| `Team OK` | Ready for team driving - CALL THEM! |
| `Solo Only` | Wants solo work - does NOT qualify |
| `Done` | Called |
| `Sent link` | IntelliApp link sent |
