TRANSPORTATION OF PHARMACEUTICALS

The pharmaceutical industry is vital for our national health, and Divine Enterprises understands that reliable transportation connections between production facilities are important. We take pride in providing services to some of the largest pharmaceutical transportation companies in the world. In fact, as a member of the Pharmaceutical Cargo Security Coalition, Divine Enterprises always receives the latest industry news and is proactive in terms of protecting your freight.

 

  • We realize the importance of temperature stability in maintaining the integrity of life science products, all the way from manufacturers to end customers, and we strive for perfection with every load.
  • All of our refrigeration units are equipped with state-of- the-art devices that continuously track and record real-time temperature readings and analytics, engine conditions and engine fault monitoring. During transit, our system monitors set point, ambient temperature, supply air temperature, return air temperature, reefer unit status/mode, door status, battery voltage and fuel level.

TRANSPORTATION TEMPERATURE CONTROLLED

TRANSPORTATION OF HAZMAT MATERIALS

Hazmat materials need to be handled with precautionary measures by drivers with the highest qualifications, who are well-trained and carefully selected in accordance with the highest standards of employment and comprehensive background checks. 

 
Divine Enterprises has considerable experience in the hazmat market segment, constantly working on drivers’ safety awareness. We operate within the most stringent security guidelines to stay in compliance with the National Industrial Security Program for hazmat-classified material, which makes us the most trustworthy carrier on the market today.
 
Our extensive preventive maintenance program allows us to keep all the vehicles in excellent condition through systematic inspections, failure detection and correction of any incipient malfunctions before they become a major defect.
