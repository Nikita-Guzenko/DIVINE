FACILITY

At Divine Enterprises, we are proud of our new, state-of-the-art 40,000 sq.ft. facility that we custom built from the ground up.

 

Our headquarter is conveniently located in Greater Sacramento region, in Rocklin California.

 

OFFICE

Our office headquarters is not typical for a trucking company. Besides tasteful aesthetics, it was designed for the enjoyment and convenience of our employees and drivers. We believe this exemplifies our spirit of excellence and attention to detail in everything we do.


An impressive lobby, comfortable conference and training rooms, spectacular driver’s lounge with showers are just a few of the amenities we provide for our employees and guests.

WAREHOUSING

Our GMP & GDP Warehouse is located in its own, highly-secured, modern facility that spans 18,000 sq. ft.

 

We are fully capable of handling and storing various commodities, including pharmaceutical goods, steel constructions, machinery, and automobile parts according to GDP and ISO 9001 standards. The facility is equipped with 48 CCTV cameras and the highest security measures to ensure safe storage and handling.

 

GDP and ISO 9001 procedures provide the highest level of security while transporting high-value, and time- and temperature-critical freight.

 

SHOP

An in-house repair facility is a necessary luxury for a company of our caliber. It allows us to keep our fleet and equipment in top-notch shape. Since Divine Enterprises moves temperature-controlled, high-value freight, it’s essential to have qualified and certified mechanics at our service at any given time.


HTS (Heavy Truck Services) is Divine’s service provider that oversees our shop/repair and parts departments.

 

  • HTS is a full service center of about 14,000 sq, ft. that proudly serves many trucking companies as well as Divine’s fleet. In addition to routine maintenance and major repairs, we are certified to perform government-regulated DOT inspections, computer diagnostics, as well as deal with insurance claims and warranty work for heavy duty trucks and trailers.
  • Having four repair doors and two maintenance lines, we can simultaneously service up to 12 units.
