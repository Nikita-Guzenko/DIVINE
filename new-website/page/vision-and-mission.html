VISION & MISSION STATEMENT
VISION
TO BE RECOGNIZED AS THE NATIONAL PREMIER PROVIDER IN LOGISTICS AND TRANSPORTATION OF HIGH-VALUE FREIGHT.

Our vision has evolved. While delivering cargo safely and on time remains the foundation of our operation, we believe that doing just that is not enough anymore. We are mindful of the environmental (emission, clean air, etc.) and societal impact (creating jobs) our business has on the world. We call this idea “Transportation With Integrity” and it guides how we run our business.”

MISSION

DIVINE ENTERPRISES INC.’S, MISSION IS TO PROVIDE CLOSE TO PERFECTION CUSTOMIZED TRANSPORTATION AND LOGISTIC SERVICES IN THE NICHE MARKET OF HIGH-VALUE FREIGHT WITH A FOCUS ON SAFETY, INTEGRITY AND PROFITABILITY.

VALUES AND BELIEVES:
  • Our company name is a direct reflection of our faith, which is our first and main core value.
  • We started as a family business, but became a business family. Divine Enterprises is all about building people that build the business.
  • Drivers - you say goodbye to your familIes to supply the needs of many. Respect!
  • We are unreasonably selective about our peers, as we provide a stable work environment with equal opportunity for personal and professional growth, creating a culture of belonging.
  • Employees will be provided the same concern, respect, and caring attitude within the organization that they are expected to share externally with every Divine Enterprises customer.
  • There is only one boss. The Customer. If we don’t take care of them, someone else will.
  • Do more with less! There are no traffic jams along the extra mile
  • Have fun. Laugh, smile, innovate! Look beautiful! Life is too precious to be someplace lame.
