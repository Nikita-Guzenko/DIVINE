REPAIR FACILITY

Heavy Truck Services (HTS) is a service provider for Divine Enterprises since 2013. HTS specializes in the repair and maintenance of semi-trucks and trailers. We also have our in-house sales parts department. HTS carries an extensive inventory of parts and tires at any given time.

FULL SERVICE CENTER

Heavy Truck Services is a full-service center for heavy duty truck and trailer repairs, including routine maintenance and major repair work. We also work with various limited warranties and perform repairs related to insurance claims.

 

HTS has everything the customer needs in one visit - highly certified mechanics, commercial truck replacement parts and heavy-duty truck service and repair equipment.

Services we provide:

 
Truck Alignment – Including car haulers and low clearance heavy duty equipment
 
Tire services – Sell tires, mount and
balance tires
 
DPF Repair – Inspect, cleaning, or replacement with a new one
 
PM service – Oil change and
preventative maintenance
 
Diagnostics – Computer
diagnostics and troubleshooting
 
Refrigerator unit services – Repair
and service reefer units
 
A/C service
 
DOT inspections

BEST IN CLASS REPAIR SERVICES

HTS provides services to a diversified client base, from one truck to a large fleet. Our state-of-the-art facility is staffed with certified technicians who stay on top of the latest technology through ongoing rigorous training and the most advanced equipment and tools available on the market.

HTS truck repair is a full-service, including routine maintenance and major repair work.

 

  • Many of our services have a flat rate. The hourly rate for most repairs is $110/hour.
  • Our facility can service up to 12 units simultaneously.
