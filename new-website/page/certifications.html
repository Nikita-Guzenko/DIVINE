Compliance with the Requirements of a Leading Company

Our unique and comprehensive set of highly-coveted certifications is what differentiates Divine Enterprises from all other transportation companies. It took us years to obtain them and we are extremely proud of each and every one of them. In addition to collecting the necessary documentations and conducting countless inspections, we have also invested in the highest possible security measures when it comes to our state-of-the-art facility.

 

Divine Enterprises has implemented on-going rigorous training for our entire staff as well as our elite professional driving team in order to stay on top of the ever-changing requirements and regulations for this industry.

Certified Carrier: TAPA, GDP, ISO TSA, SMARTWAY, DUN & BRADSTREET

OUR CERTIFICATES

TAPA – TRANSPORTED ASSET PROTECTION ASSOCIATION

The TAPA TSR Certification is granted to only a few trucking companies, therefore, obtaining the status of a "TAPA Certified Carrier" is a pride for our employees. The TAPA certification shows the on-going commitment of our company to the importance of security and compliancewithin the industry. Divine Enterprises, as a TAPA member, is regarded as a leader in supply chain security. We specialize in securing transportation of high value, high risk cargo with TAPA certification:

 

  • an effective physical security system to reduce the loss of high-value assets
  • increased security of warehousing and handling

GDP – GOOD DISTRIBUTION PRACTICE

The GDP Certification for Pharmaceuticals and High-Value Products demonstrates a dedication to good distributive practices in every aspect of the provided service, from the early delivery of raw materials to the final shipment of finished drugs.

 

Internationally recognized pharmaceutical regulations require that distributors of such products align their operations with GDP standards. Therefore, having reached GDP certified carrier, Divine Enterprises is committed to being a vital partner in the healthcare supply chain.

ISO 900:2015 – CERTIFICATION FOR QUALITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Divine Enterprises initiated and successfully completed an overhaul of all its policies and procedures to satisfy the most sophisticated and strict operating standards dictated by ISO 9001:2015. ISO 9001 is defined as the highest international standard that specifies requirements for a Quality Management System (QMS).

 

ISO Certified Carrier use the standard to demonstrate the ability to consistently provide services that meet customer and regulatory requirements.

DUN & BRADSTREET VERIFIED - BUSINESS CREDIBILITY

Divine Enterprises is verified by one of the most credible credit rating companies in the world. Dun & Bradstreet is a data-driven risk management and scoring company that evaluates a company’s financial stress score, delinquency predictor score, and supplier evaluation risk rating.

 

The DUNS number is the gold standard in validating corporate entities on a global scale. The VERIFIED™ Business Report showcases Divine Enterprises’ business credibility.

SMARTWAY - SUSTAINABILITY THROUGH TRANSPORTATION EFFICIENCY

Fewer than 10% of all SmartWay carriers operate fleets efficient enough to make the SmartWay High Performer list for carbon emissions. Divine Enterprises is proud to be on that list.

 

SmartWay has recognized Divine Enterprises for our leadership in moving goods using less fuel, more efficiently and with a reduced carbon footprint, which sends a clear signal to suppliers, customers and the broader public about a company’s environmental commitment.
